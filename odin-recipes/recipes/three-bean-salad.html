Three Bean Salad

Description

Three-bean salad is a classic side dish featuring a medley of three types of beans (typically green beans, kidney beans, and wax beans) marinated in a tangy vinaigrette with onions and sometimes other vegetables, creating a refreshing and colorful combination.

Ingredients

  • 1 (15 ounce) can green beans
  • 1 pound wax beans
  • 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 onion, sliced into thin rings
  • ¾ cup white sugar, or to taste
  • ⅔ cup distilled white vinegar
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon celery seed

steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mix together green beans, wax beans, kidney beans, onion, sugar, vinegar, vegetable oil, salt, pepper, and celery seed. Chill in refrigerator for at least 12 hours.
  3. Enjoy!
