Berserk

The Berserk manga and anime series features a cast of characters created by Kentaro Miura. The series takes place in a dark fantasy setting loosely based on medieval Europe. Berserk centers on the life of Guts, a lone mercenary warrior, and Griffith, the leader of a mercenary band called the Band of the Hawk who made a pact with demons and is reborn as one himself.

Main Characters

Guts, renowned as the "Black Swordsman" , is a former mercenary and branded wanderer who travels the world in a constant internal struggle between pursuing his own ends and upholding his attachments to those dear to him.

Casca is a former unit commander of the Band of the Falcon and an estranged companion of Guts. Casca proves to be a determined and capable leader within the Band of the Falcon who merits the respect of her comrades in arms

Griffith is the current leader of the reborn Band of the Falcon and supreme commander of the Midland Regular Army. Having been at the fore of many battles, he has amassed a reputation as a savior across the continent, and is revered as the "Falcon of Light".

Puck is an elf of the Pisky race, descendants of the ancient spirits of wind. A former denizen of Elfhelm, the idyllic elven realm of the Flower Storm Monarch, he left out of sheer boredom and became Guts' first newfound companion since.
